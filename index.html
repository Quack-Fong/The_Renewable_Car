The Renewable Car

The Car To Power Your Future

Applications


The Renewable Car will lead us to a future where having to drop by for gas becomes a scenario of the past. The renewable car will be able to run, theoretically, forever since the car will be able to get solar energy from the sun while also storing compressed air from the air around it. This way the car will be able to run on both sources of energy whenever it needs to. It solves the problem of requiring stronger and more expensive solar panels to have cars run longer. Instead you would simply need two inexpensive systems that work together, this resulting in a car that solves both the problems of increasing gas prices as well as pollution. Overall, the renewable car is a revolutionary product that will lead us to a brighter and longer lasting future.

Benefits

  1. Costs less money!
    2. You will never need to pay for gas or spend any time charging your car at any outlet. More time for you, more money for you.




  2. Healthier for the environment
    3. Because of its use of both solar energy and wind energy, the car won't need to release any unhealthy gases into the air. This leads to less polluted airs and healthier lives for everyone.




  3. Longer Lasting Car
    4. Being able to collect energy during the day as well as during rides, this car can theoretically run forever. It will just never run out of energy. Even if you decide to not drive it at all throughout the day, the car will still have a ton of energy stored from just the sun. This car will be ready whenever you are ready.
